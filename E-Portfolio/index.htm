About Me

My name is Reko Smith and I currently study Games Design and Development at Manchester Metropolitan University. I am also the Chairman of the Manchester Young Carers Group, Executive Member of the Manchester Youth Council, Steering Group Member at the Children's Rights Alliance for England and National Participation Group Member for NSPCC.

Why did I choose this course?

I love video games. In fact, my favourite genre of video games is RPG/JRPG fantasy games because of the way they're able to portray a story, the level of design on the characters, the world, the attention to detail, the amount of creativity in the gameplay, etc. I would like to replicate this, and by pursuing my dream goal of being a game designer, I will be one step closer to making my game.
